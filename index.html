What you'll learn and build

  • Performing exploratory analysis using pandas, NumPy and Matplotlib, etc.
  • How to define the right questions to choose and drive your analysis.
  • Data cleaning methods.
  • Descriptive statistics - mean, median mode, measurement of spread, standard deviation, percentile, variance, skewness, correlation, etc.
  • Sampling distribution, Gaussian and summary statistics, Central Limit theorem.
  • Inferential statistics - hypothesis testing, significance tests, type 1 & 2 errors, p-value, and confidence intervals.

Training Offerings

  • Deeply curated content based on the applicability of the topics
  • Build projects and break down case studies to learn how to apply statistics to solve data science problems.
  • Code along to implement the learned concepts using Python.
  • Take-away exercises.
  • Career tips - how to prepare for interviews, jobs to look for, good companies that are looking for Analysts/Scientists/Engineers.
  • Recorded video of the session.

Who should attend this training session

You are a programmer or an aspiring data analyst/scientist.

For all the beginners in the field of Data/ML/AI with some familiarity with elementary mathematics, and python programming.

Prerequisites

  • Python Programming, Pandas, Matplotlib.
  • Basic Mathematics(Arithmetics, notations, percentages, etc.) and Statistics(average, standard deviation, variance, tabular data, etc.)
  • Familiar with basic statistics necessary.
